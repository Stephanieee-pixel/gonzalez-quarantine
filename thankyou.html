A special thank you

During the COVID-19 pandemic many people have been risking their lives in order to make sure we're safe during this quarantine.

Previous Next



Healthcare

These people have risked their lives to attend people who have caught the virus. They work day and night to do everything they can to help patients survive

Teachers

Many teachers have been putting in a lot of effort to continue giving us an education. They do their best to make sure we are confortable with our remote learning. They help us through every step.

Researchers

There are many researchers out there that are working hard and nonstop to find a cure for the COVID-19 virus. We thank you all for putting in all your time and effort into ending this pandemic soon.

Grocery Store Workers

All grocery store workers are also putting themselves at risk when they go to work to make sure that their communites have food, supplies, and other essentials they need. Everyone thanks you for your hard work.
